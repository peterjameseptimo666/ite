About Nexle Sweet Delight

It all began in the quiet warmth of a small kitchen in Caloocan City, where Ms. Monet L. Denoso first discovered her love for baking. What started as a simple hobby of making cupcakes for family gatherings and birthday surprises. Slowly blossomed into something more meaningful. Each whisk, each sprinkle of flour, became an act of joy and creativity.

During her early days, Ms. Monet would spend late nights perfecting recipes using only the simplest tools she had. Her neighbors soon began to notice the sweet aroma wafting from her home and started placing small orders for celebrations. With every satisfied customer, her confidence grew, and so did her dream to share her homemade creations with more people.
